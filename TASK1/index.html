Who We Are

About InAmigos Foundation

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organisation founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO). Based in Chhattisgarh, the organisation works across India to create sustainable social impact through community-driven initiatives and volunteer engagement.

What began as a small volunteer group has grown into a nationwide movement — touching thousands of lives through education, environmental action, animal welfare, women empowerment, and skill development. Every initiative is built on the belief that collective action creates lasting change.

Recognised by the Government of India and certified under multiple regulatory frameworks, InAmigos Foundation operates with complete transparency and a deep commitment to measurable, community-first impact.

Our Mission

To build a more inclusive, compassionate, and empowered society through collective action and meaningful social initiatives.
